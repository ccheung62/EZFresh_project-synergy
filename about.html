Why EZ Fresh?

EZFresh is affordable and convenient.

  • With our services, you don’t have to worry about moving your schedule around to get the best breakfast, lunch, or dinner.
  • Shop from local supermarkets you know and love while supporting your community!
  • Buy from however many supermarkets you want for the best price and quality without the carrying and traveling!
  • This is a service for everyone! If you can't figure out how to order online, you can contact us through customer services. Doesn't matter how early or late it is, our team will be eager to help you or order for you!
  • Chiefs of different experiences and cultures can promote their recipes to the world and enhance healthier eating!
It’s affordable, efficient, and reliant. Go EZ on your groceries, go FRESH with your ingredients!

Our Mission

EZFresh was founded by a group of Diverse Influencers Fellows during the pandemic with the idea of

  • making grocery shopping convenient and safe for our community
  • helping vulnerable groups, such as the elderly and disabled, get their groceries and food to eat healthy.
  • maintaining social distancing within supermarkets and low COVID cases
So, we decided to build a more affordable, day-to-day grocery shopping delivery system. Now, you can schedule our services and our team will make sure you and your love ones live and eat healthy.
